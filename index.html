How to Play - Airport Manager

üéØ Objective

Build and manage an airport to earn money. Expand your airport with new buildings and upgrades to maximize income and passenger flow.

üèóÔ∏è Building Your Airport

  1. Select a building from the Buildings panel on the right
  2. Click on the grass to place it at that location
  3. You need a Runway for planes to land and take off
  4. You need a Terminal to count passengers from landing planes
  5. Add support buildings (Hangar, Fuel Station, etc.) to increase efficiency

üí∞ Making Money

Each building generates income per second. The more buildings you have, the faster your income grows.

  • Terminal: $50/sec (handles passengers)
  • Runway: $100/sec (enables flights)
  • Hangar: $75/sec (stores aircraft)
  • Fuel Station: $40/sec (increases plane frequency)

‚úàÔ∏è Planes & Passengers

Planes automatically arrive and depart based on your runways:

  • Each runway spawns 2 planes per hour (scales with upgrades)
  • Planes approach from the left and land on your runway
  • Blue particles appear when planes land
  • If you have a Terminal, each landing brings 50-150 passengers
  • Planes depart after 2 seconds on the ground with orange flame particles

‚¨ÜÔ∏è Upgrading Buildings

Right-click on any building to upgrade it:

  • Each upgrade costs 50% of the original building cost √ó current level
  • Upgrades increase income by 30% per level
  • Level indicators show as "Lv2", "Lv3" etc. on buildings
  • Higher level buildings generate more income

üéÆ Controls

Left Click: Select building (right panel), then click map to place
Right Click: Upgrade a building (increases its income by 30%)

üí° Tips for Success

  • Start by building 1-2 Terminals and 1 Runway to get income flowing
  • Upgrade buildings regularly - it's often more profitable than building new ones
  • Balance Terminals and Runways - you need both for passengers
  • Add support buildings like Hangars and Fuel Stations for synergy
  • Plan your airport layout - buildings don't just look good, they show their income!
  • Watch the status overlay (top-left) to see your money and active planes

üìä Game Stats

  • Money: Your current funds
  • Income/sec: How much money you earn per second
